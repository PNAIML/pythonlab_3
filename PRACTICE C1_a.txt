Here's a set of **20 multiple-choice questions (MCQs)** covering machine learning and its types: **Supervised Learning, Unsupervised Learning, and Reinforcement Learning**.

---

### **1. General Machine Learning**  
**Question:** What is the primary goal of machine learning?  
A) To enable computers to learn from data without explicit programming  
B) To replace human intelligence completely  
C) To develop only rule-based systems  
D) To eliminate all forms of human interaction  

**Correct Answer:** **A) To enable computers to learn from data without explicit programming**  

---

### **2. Supervised Learning**  
**Question:** Which of the following is a key characteristic of supervised learning?  
A) Training with labeled data  
B) No predefined categories for data  
C) Learning through trial and error  
D) Data is randomly assigned  

**Correct Answer:** **A) Training with labeled data**  

---

### **3. Unsupervised Learning**  
**Question:** What is the main objective of unsupervised learning?  
A) To learn patterns and structures from unlabeled data  
B) To follow strict rules given by programmers  
C) To only predict future trends  
D) To perform reinforcement-based tasks  

**Correct Answer:** **A) To learn patterns and structures from unlabeled data**  

---

### **4. Reinforcement Learning**  
**Question:** Reinforcement learning is mainly based on which principle?  
A) Learning from rewards and penalties  
B) Using labeled datasets for training  
C) Assigning pre-defined clusters to data  
D) Ignoring previous experiences  

**Correct Answer:** **A) Learning from rewards and penalties**
